About Khadija Dauda Care Foundation

We are committed to helping wounded kids find their place.

Khadija Dauda Care Foundation is a not for-profit and non-political organization that aim to provide assistance to orphans and the vulnerable children; special care for girl child education.

We also promote self reliance through human development and entrepreneural skills, create awareness in communities by social and environmental programmes.

Our Mission

Orphans Care

Our main mission is to provide orphans and vulnerable children, their welfare and education development.

Self reliance

We aim to promote self reliance through human development and entrepreneural skills.

Good Governance

We also aim to promote good governance through leadership development programmes and youth empowerment innitiative.

Awareness

Disseminate information to society/communities through social and environmental programmes.
